Snacks Menu

  • Momos

    Momos are dumplings originating from Nepal and Tibet, filled with a mixture of vegetables, meats, or both, and wrapped in thin dough. They're often steamed or fried and served with spicy dipping sauces, offering a delicious and flavorful snack or meal option.

    Rs.80

  • Pizza

    Pizza is a classic Italian dish consisting of a round, flattened base of dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings like vegetables, meats, and herbs. Baked in an oven, it delivers a perfect balance of flavors and textures, making it a beloved comfort food enjoyed worldwide.

    Rs.100

  • Burger

    A burger is a sandwich typically consisting of a cooked patty of ground meat (such as beef, chicken, or plant-based alternatives), placed inside a sliced bun and often accompanied by various toppings like lettuce, tomato, cheese, onions, pickles, and condiments like ketchup or mayonnaise, creating a satisfying and filling meal.

    Rs.70

  • Popcorn

    Popcorn is a popular snack made from dried corn kernels that puff up when heated. Light and airy, it's typically seasoned with salt or butter, but can also be flavored with cheese, caramel, or various spices, providing a satisfying crunch and a burst of flavor with every bite.

    Rs.40

  • Gobi Manchuria

    Gobi Manchurian is a popular Indo-Chinese dish made from cauliflower florets coated in a batter, deep-fried until crispy, and then tossed in a flavorful sauce made with garlic, ginger, soy sauce, and other seasonings. It offers a delightful combination of crispy texture and tangy-sweet flavors, perfect as an appetizer or main dish.

    Rs.60

  • Chicken Manchuria

    Chicken Manchurian is a Chinese-inspired dish featuring bite-sized pieces of chicken coated in a spicy, tangy sauce made with soy sauce, vinegar, chili sauce, and garlic. Served hot and garnished with green onions, it's a delicious and satisfying dish often enjoyed with rice or noodles.

    Rs.80

  • Chicken Shawarma

    Chicken Shawarma is a Middle Eastern dish made from thinly sliced marinated chicken cooked on a vertical rotisserie. Served wrapped in pita bread or flatbread with garlic sauce, pickles, tomatoes, and lettuce, it offers a flavorful and aromatic experience, with tender chicken and a variety of complementary flavors.

    Rs.120

  • Panneer Shawarma

    Paneer Shawarma is a vegetarian version of the classic Middle Eastern dish, substituting marinated paneer cheese for the meat. The paneer is grilled or roasted until golden brown and served in a similar manner to chicken shawarma, providing a tasty and satisfying alternative for vegetarians and cheese lovers alike.

    Rs.100
